About this game

It's a modified version of classical game Bulls and Cows. One of participants thinks of a number which consists of non-repeatable digits, and the second participant tries to guess the number proposing variants. The first gamer compares the original number and the proposed one and reports to the second gamer two values: the overall amount of the coincident digits (cows) and the amount of the coincident digits which have the right position (bulls).
