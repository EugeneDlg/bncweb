About this game

Welcome to Bulls and Cows game! It's a slightly modified version of the classical variant of Bulls and Cows. The rules are very simple:one of participants thinks of a number which consists of non-repeatable digits, and the second participant tries to guess the number proposing variants. The first gamer compares the original number and the proposed one and reports to the second gamer two values: the overall amount of the coincident digits (Cows) and the amount of the coincident digits which have the right position (Bulls).

In settings you can choose Dual game i.e. I will think of a number for you and you will try to guess it and at the same time you will think of your number for me and I will try guess it:

Or Single game so that I will be trying to guess your number only. It's less interesting, but I can impress you how quick I can find your number :)

Also in settings you are able to choose the amount of digits in my and your numbers. Usually it's 4, but you can experiment with other amount.

Also you can look at the fixture list where history of results of other games is stored.

Please read more about the game rules and other stuff at the following link: Bulls and cows


Good luck!
